Who We Are

Gesleap is a newly formed technology company focused on how humans interact with the computers. We specialize in converting hand motions and gestures into input. Unlike other competitors, the only hardware required is a standard webcam. We leverage computer vision algorithms joined with machine learning techniques to accurately track hand movements and translate them into commands.

Our Mission

We envision a world where computers respond to intuitive human movement seamlessly and effortlessly. Our mission is to develop cutting-edge gesture recognition technology that:

  • Can be set up within minutes.
  • Works reliably on a wide range of devices without additional hardware.
  • Can be tailored to the customer's application needs.
